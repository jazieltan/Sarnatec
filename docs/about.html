Milestones

1997
Swissbuiltec AG starts activities in SE Asia with Simon Fluck as Technical Manager providing technical expertise for the design, fabrication and installation of custom made metal roofing systems and trading of roofing products.

2003
Founding of SWISSPROF Sdn. Bhd. with Simon Fluck as Technical Director. Swissprof’s main activities are the design, supply & installation of custom made metal roofing systems.

2012
Completion of more than 150,000m2 of Sika Sarnafil Membrane Roofing Systems in Malaysia & Vietnam. Forming of Sarnatec Sdn Bhd as a specialist Single Ply Membrane Roofing Company – continuation of Swissprof business under Sarnatec Sdn Bhd.
